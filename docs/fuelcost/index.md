# Privacy Policy Fuel Cost Calculator

**Effective Date: 24 March 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Fuel Cost Calculator app ("the App") handles your information. The Developer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is straightforward: the App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioural data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Device identifiers
- Financial or payment information
- Any other data that could be used to identify you

---

## 3. How the App Works

All calculations performed by the App take place entirely on your device. The values you enter (fuel efficiency, fuel price, and trip distance) are used solely to compute an estimated fuel cost and are never transmitted anywhere. The only data saved between sessions is your last-used input values and unit preferences, which are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism and never leave your device.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. No data is shared with any third party.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no data at risk. All locally stored preferences remain on your device and are subject to Apple's own device security measures.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated in the future, the revised policy will be made available through the App Store listing. The effective date at the top of this document will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Developer via the App Store support link on the Fuel Cost Calculator listing page.
